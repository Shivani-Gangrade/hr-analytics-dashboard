## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition trends using an HR dataset.
The goal is to uncover key factors influencing employee turnover and provide
data-driven insights to improve retention strategies.

The analysis is performed using **Power BI**, leveraging interactive dashboards
to explore employee demographics, salary distribution, job roles, and tenure.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Identify key drivers of employee attrition
* Analyze attrition patterns across age groups and salary bands
* Understand which job roles have highest turnover
* Evaluate impact of experience (years at company) on attrition
* Help HR teams design targeted retention strategies

## 📈 Key Insights

### 👥 Age & Attrition

* Highest attrition in **26–35 age group**
* Early-career employees are more likely to leave

### 💰 Salary Impact

* Majority attrition occurs in **salary ≤ 5K**
* Higher salary bands show lower attrition

### 💼 Job Role Analysis

* **Laboratory Technician, Sales Executive, Research Scientist** show highest attrition
* Indicates role-specific pressure or dissatisfaction

### ⏳ Experience (Years at Company)

* Peak attrition occurs in **0–3 years**
* Suggests onboarding and early engagement issues

### 🎓 Education

* Employees from **Life Sciences & Medical fields** have higher attrition

### ⚧ Gender

* Slightly higher attrition among **male employees**

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Improve retention strategies for **early-tenure employees**
* Re-evaluate compensation for **low salary bands**
* Focus on high-risk roles with targeted HR interventions
* Strengthen onboarding and engagement programs

## 🚀 How To Use

1. Download the `.pbix` file from the `dashboard/` folder  
2. Open in **Power BI Desktop**  
3. Interact with filters (Department, Gender, Job Role)  
4. Use **Drill-through (right-click)** on visuals to explore detailed insights  
5. Analyze trends and patterns  

---

## 🔍 Drill-through Functionality

This dashboard includes an interactive **drill-through feature**:

* Users can right-click on any visual (e.g., Job Role, Age Group, Department)
* Selecting **Drill-through** navigates to a detailed view
* The **Employee Details page is hidden by default**
* It is only accessible through drill-through interactions

👉 This enables users to perform **deep-dive analysis on specific segments** without cluttering the main dashboard.
